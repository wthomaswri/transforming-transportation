Reimagining Safe and Resilient Mobility For Recovery

February 3—5, 2021

Registration Coming Soon

The COVID-19 pandemic has disrupted the global transport sector and the people and businesses that rely on it in unprecedented ways. Across the globe, rethinking mobility is now a priority to build back better, with safer, more resilient and efficient transport systems for all.

Transforming Transportation 2021 will bring together sustainable mobility world leaders from the public, private, academic and civil society spheres in a global virtual event to discuss the path forward.

More details will be announced in the coming weeks. We are committed to fully utilizing the new virtual format to gather and engage with an even more diverse audience from across the globe, and to offer more resources on pressing issues related to sustainable transportation and mobility.

We look forward to seeing you (virtually) in February 2021!
